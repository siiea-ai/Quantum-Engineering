## Synthesis Methods

### Thematic Synthesis
- Group papers by topic rather than chronology
- Identify recurring themes across studies
- Note how themes evolve over time

### Matrix Analysis
- Compare papers across standardized dimensions
- Identify what each paper contributes
- Find where papers complement or contradict

### Meta-Synthesis
- Integrate qualitative findings
- Build higher-order interpretations
- Develop explanatory frameworks

## Key Synthesis Questions

1. **Agreement:** What do most papers agree on?
2. **Debate:** Where are there active disagreements?
3. **Evolution:** How has understanding changed over time?
4. **Methods:** Which methods produce which insights?
5. **Gaps:** What questions remain unanswered?
