## Gap Types

### Empirical Gaps
- Missing experimental validation
- Untested predictions
- Limited parameter ranges explored
- Insufficient replication

### Theoretical Gaps
- Unexplained observations
- Conflicting theoretical predictions
- Missing analytical frameworks
- Incomplete models

### Methodological Gaps
- Inadequate measurement techniques
- Need for new analysis methods
- Scalability limitations
- Precision/accuracy limitations

### Application Gaps
- Unexplored practical implementations
- Missing real-world validation
- Technology transfer opportunities
- Commercial/industrial needs

### Population/Context Gaps
- Understudied systems or materials
- Different parameter regimes
- Novel conditions or environments
- Cross-domain applications

## Gap Evaluation Criteria

### Significance Assessment

| Criterion | High | Medium | Low |
|-----------|------|--------|-----|
| **Impact** | Paradigm-shifting | Important advance | Incremental |
| **Scope** | Field-wide | Sub-field | Narrow niche |
| **Urgency** | Blocking progress | Important | Nice to have |
| **Interest** | High community interest | Moderate | Limited |

### Feasibility Assessment

| Criterion | High | Medium | Low |
|-----------|------|--------|-----|
| **Resources** | Available | Obtainable | Prohibitive |
| **Expertise** | Have it | Can develop | Major barrier |
| **Timeline** | Months | 1-2 years | Many years |
| **Risk** | Low technical risk | Moderate | High |

## Gap Validation Methods

1. **Literature Check**: Ensure gap hasn't been recently addressed
2. **Expert Consultation**: Discuss with advisors and peers
3. **Conference Review**: Check recent conference proceedings
4. **Preprint Search**: Check arXiv for in-progress work
5. **Community Assessment**: Is this a recognized need?

## Common Pitfalls

### Pitfall 1: Obvious Gaps
**Problem**: Identifying gaps everyone knows about
**Solution**: Dig deeper; find second-order gaps

### Pitfall 2: Infeasible Gaps
**Problem**: Gaps requiring unavailable resources
**Solution**: Consider your actual capabilities

### Pitfall 3: Trivial Gaps
**Problem**: Gaps that don't matter
**Solution**: Always ask "so what?"

### Pitfall 4: Already Addressed
**Problem**: Recent work you missed
**Solution**: Check preprints and recent proceedings

### Pitfall 5: Too Many Gaps
**Problem**: Identifying every possible gap
**Solution**: Prioritize ruthlessly
