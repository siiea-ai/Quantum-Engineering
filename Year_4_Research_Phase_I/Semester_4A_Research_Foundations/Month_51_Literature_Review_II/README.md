## Key Concepts

### Literature Review Types

1. **Narrative Review**: Traditional, selective discussion of literature
2. **Systematic Review**: Comprehensive, reproducible methodology
3. **Scoping Review**: Maps available evidence on a topic
4. **Meta-Analysis**: Quantitative synthesis of results
5. **Integrative Review**: Combines diverse methodologies

### Gap Types

- **Empirical Gaps**: Missing experimental evidence
- **Theoretical Gaps**: Unexplained phenomena or conflicting theories
- **Methodological Gaps**: Need for new techniques or approaches
- **Population/Context Gaps**: Understudied domains or conditions
- **Application Gaps**: Unexplored practical implementations
