# Paper Annotation Template

## Instructions

Use this template to create structured annotations for papers you read in depth. Consistent annotation enables:
- Quick retrieval of key information later
- Comparison across papers
- Identification of patterns and gaps
- Building your research knowledge base

**Time Budget:** 10-15 minutes per paper (after reading)
