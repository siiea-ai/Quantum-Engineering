# Seminal Papers by Research Area

## Overview

This resource organizes foundational and influential papers by research area to support your Week 194 deep dives. For each area, papers are categorized as:

- **Foundational:** Established the field or key concept
- **Breakthrough:** Major advance that shifted the field
- **State-of-Art:** Current best results or methods
- **Review:** Comprehensive surveys for efficient coverage

Use this as a starting point; expand based on your specific interests within each area.

## 1. Quantum Algorithms

### Variational Quantum Algorithms

**Foundational:**
1. **VQE Original Paper**
   - Peruzzo et al., "A variational eigenvalue solver on a photonic quantum processor" (2014)
   - Nature Communications 5, 4213
   - *Introduced VQE for molecular simulation*

2. **QAOA Original Paper**
   - Farhi, Goldstone, Gutmann, "A Quantum Approximate Optimization Algorithm" (2014)
   - arXiv:1411.4028
   - *Introduced QAOA for combinatorial optimization*

**Breakthrough:**
3. **Hardware-Efficient Ansatz**
   - Kandala et al., "Hardware-efficient variational quantum eigensolver for small molecules and quantum magnets" (2017)
   - Nature 549, 242
   - *Demonstrated practical VQE on superconducting qubits*

4. **Barren Plateaus Discovery**
   - McClean et al., "Barren plateaus in quantum neural network training landscapes" (2018)
   - Nature Communications 9, 4812
   - *Identified key trainability challenge*

**State-of-Art:**
5. **ADAPT-VQE**
   - Grimsley et al., "An adaptive variational algorithm for exact molecular simulations on a quantum computer" (2019)
   - Nature Communications 10, 3007

**Review:**
6. **Cerezo et al., "Variational quantum algorithms" (2021)**, Nature Reviews Physics 3, 625
