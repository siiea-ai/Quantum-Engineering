## Appendix: Evaluation Rubric

Your statement will be evaluated against these criteria:

| Criterion | Excellent (4) | Good (3) | Adequate (2) | Needs Work (1) |
|-----------|---------------|----------|--------------|----------------|
| **Problem Clarity** | Crystal clear, specific problem | Clear problem | Somewhat vague | Unclear |
| **Importance** | Compelling significance | Well-motivated | Some motivation | Not motivated |
| **Approach** | Specific, plausible approach | Clear directions | Some ideas | No approach |
| **Personal Fit** | Strong demonstrated fit | Good fit | Some fit | No fit shown |
| **Writing Quality** | Polished, engaging | Clear, professional | Adequate | Poor |
| **Structure** | Excellent flow | Good organization | Some issues | Disorganized |
