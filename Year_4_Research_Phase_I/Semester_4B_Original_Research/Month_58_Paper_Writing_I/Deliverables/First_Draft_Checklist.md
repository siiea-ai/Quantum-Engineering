# Month 58 Deliverable: First Draft Manuscript Checklist

## Overview

This checklist tracks the completion of your first draft manuscript. Use it to ensure all required components are complete before moving to the revision phase in Month 59.

**Month:** 58 - Paper Writing I
**Days:** 1597-1624
**Goal:** Complete first draft of research paper ready for revision

### Deliverables

## Week 230: Methods & Results Sections

### Deliverables

- [ ] **Methods Section Draft**
  - [ ] System/model description complete
  - [ ] Experimental apparatus or computational setup described
  - [ ] Measurement protocol or numerical methods explained
  - [ ] Data analysis procedures documented
  - [ ] Reproducibility standard met

- [ ] **Results Section Draft**
  - [ ] All major results presented
  - [ ] Quantitative values with uncertainties throughout
  - [ ] Figures referenced in logical order
  - [ ] Objective presentation (no interpretation)
  - [ ] Negative results included if relevant

- [ ] **Figures**
  - [ ] All figures created/refined
  - [ ] Figures meet quality standards (300+ DPI)
  - [ ] Consistent style across figures
  - [ ] Colorblind-accessible

- [ ] **Figure Captions**
  - [ ] All captions written
  - [ ] Captions are self-contained
  - [ ] Symbols and colors defined
  - [ ] Error bar meanings specified
