# Week 240: Year 4 Completion

## Overview

**Days:** 1674-1680
**Theme:** Year 4 Wrap-up and Year 5 Transition Planning

Week 240 marks the culmination of Year 4: Research Phase I. This final week focuses on comprehensive year assessment, portfolio compilation, Year 5 research proposal development, and preparation for the transition to advanced research.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this week, you will be able to:

1. Conduct comprehensive year-end self-assessment
2. Compile a research portfolio documenting Year 4 achievements
3. Develop a detailed Year 5 research proposal
4. Prepare materials for advisor meeting
5. Create a transition plan for Year 5
6. Celebrate achievements and set intentions for next phase

## Key Activities

### 1. Comprehensive Year Assessment

Evaluate Year 4 across dimensions:
- Research accomplishments
- Skill development
- Professional growth
- Personal development

### 2. Portfolio Compilation

Gather all Year 4 outputs:
- Published/submitted papers
- Presentations and posters
- Code repositories
- Data packages
- Documentation

### 3. Year 5 Research Proposal

Develop a concrete plan including:
- Research objectives
- Methodology
- Timeline
- Milestones
- Resources needed

### 4. Transition Planning

Prepare for Year 5:
- Administrative transitions
- System setup
- Learning goals
- Collaboration plans
