# Month 1 Progress Report Template

## Purpose

This template provides the official format for your Month 1 (Research Execution I) progress report. This document serves as:
- A formal checkpoint for your research project
- Documentation for your advisor and committee
- A self-assessment and planning tool
- A foundation for your eventual thesis documentation

---

## Quick Reference

**Report Length:** 15-20 pages (excluding appendices)
**Due Date:** End of Week 220 (Day 1540)
**Submission:** Share with advisor before meeting
**Review:** Discuss at advisor meeting

## Formatting Guidelines

### Document Format
- **Font:** 11pt serif (Times New Roman) or 10pt sans-serif (Arial)
- **Margins:** 1 inch all sides
- **Spacing:** 1.5 line spacing for body text
- **Headings:** Clear hierarchy with consistent formatting
- **Page numbers:** Bottom center

### Figures and Tables
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI minimum
- **Captions:** Below figures, above tables
- **Numbering:** Sequential (Figure 1, Figure 2, etc.)
- **References:** Cite all figures/tables in text

### Writing Style
- **Tense:** Past tense for completed work, present for ongoing
- **Voice:** Active voice preferred
- **Clarity:** Technical but accessible to committee
- **Honesty:** Candid about challenges and limitations
