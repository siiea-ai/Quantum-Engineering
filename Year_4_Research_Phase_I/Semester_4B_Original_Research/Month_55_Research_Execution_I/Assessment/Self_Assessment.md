# Self-Assessment: Month 55 Research Execution I

## Purpose

This self-assessment provides a structured framework for evaluating your performance during Month 55, the first month of active research execution. Honest self-assessment is crucial for:

1. Identifying strengths to leverage
2. Recognizing areas for improvement
3. Planning professional development
4. Preparing for advisor discussions
5. Documenting growth over time

Complete this assessment at the end of Week 220 (Day 1540).

---

## Assessment Instructions

- Rate each dimension on a 1-5 scale with specific evidence
- Be honest - this is for your development
- Focus on specific examples, not general impressions
- Consider both successes and challenges
- Use the "Evidence" column to justify your rating

**Rating Scale:**
| Rating | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| 1 | Unsatisfactory - Major problems, needs immediate attention |
| 2 | Needs Improvement - Significant gaps, requires focused effort |
| 3 | Satisfactory - Meets expectations, room for growth |
| 4 | Good - Exceeds expectations in most areas |
| 5 | Excellent - Outstanding performance, exemplary |
