## Exam Coverage

### From Month 40: QI/QC Theory Review I
- Density matrices: pure vs mixed, Bloch sphere, trace operations
- Composite systems: tensor products, partial trace, Schmidt decomposition
- Entanglement: separability, Bell states, entanglement measures
- Quantum channels: CPTP maps, Kraus operators, Choi-Jamiolkowski

### From Month 41: QI/QC Theory Review II
- Quantum gates: Pauli, Clifford, T, rotation gates
- Universality: gate sets, Solovay-Kitaev theorem
- Algorithms: Deutsch-Jozsa, Simon, QFT, phase estimation, Shor, Grover
- Variational algorithms: VQE, QAOA

### From Month 42: QI/QC Theory Review III
- Complexity: BQP, QMA, Local Hamiltonian, query complexity
- Protocols: teleportation, superdense coding, BB84, E91, blind QC
- Information theory: von Neumann entropy, Holevo bound, channel capacity
