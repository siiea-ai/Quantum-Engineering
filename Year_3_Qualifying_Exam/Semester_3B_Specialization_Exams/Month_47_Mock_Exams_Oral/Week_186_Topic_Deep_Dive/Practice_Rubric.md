# Presentation Practice Rubric

## Self-Assessment Criteria for Your 20-Minute Presentation

## Instructions

Use this rubric to evaluate each practice run of your presentation. Be honest and critical - the goal is to improve before the mock exam. Rate each dimension on the 1-5 scale, then calculate your total score.
