## Appendix: Quick Reference for Oral Exams

### Must-Know Formulas

**QM:**
- $[\hat{x}, \hat{p}] = i\hbar$
- $E_n^{(1)} = \langle n|V|n\rangle$
- $\hat{J}_\pm|j,m\rangle = \hbar\sqrt{j(j+1)-m(m\pm 1)}|j,m\pm 1\rangle$

**QI/QC:**
- $S(\rho) = -\text{Tr}(\rho\log_2\rho)$
- Grover: $O(\sqrt{N})$ queries
- $|\Phi^+\rangle = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(|00\rangle + |11\rangle)$

**QEC:**
- Knill-Laflamme: $PE_i^\dagger E_j P = \alpha_{ij}P$
- Threshold: $p_L \sim (p/p_{th})^{d/2}$
- Surface code: $n \approx 2d^2$
