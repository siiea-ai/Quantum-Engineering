## Appendix: Formula Quick Reference

### Must Know for Comprehensive

**QM:**
- Uncertainty: $\Delta A \Delta B \geq \frac{1}{2}|\langle[A,B]\rangle|$
- Perturbation: $E^{(1)} = \langle n|V|n\rangle$, $E^{(2)} = \sum_{k\neq n} \frac{|\langle k|V|n\rangle|^2}{E_n - E_k}$

**QI/QC:**
- Entropy: $S(\rho) = -\text{Tr}(\rho \log_2 \rho)$
- CHSH: $|S| \leq 2$ classically, $\leq 2\sqrt{2}$ quantum
- Grover: $O(\sqrt{N})$ queries

**QEC:**
- Knill-Laflamme: $PE_i^\dagger E_j P = \alpha_{ij} P$
- Threshold: $p_L \sim (p/p_{th})^{2^k}$
- Surface code: $d = L$, $n \approx 2L^2$
