# Year 4 Research Planning

## Overview

This document outlines the detailed plan for Year 4, the first year of the research phase. It translates the research proposal developed in Week 190 into actionable quarterly and monthly plans.

## Part 1: Year 4 Framework

### Research Phase Structure

```
Year 4: Research Initiation
├── Q1 (Months 49-51): Literature Mastery & Initial Research
├── Q2 (Months 52-54): First Research Project
├── Q3 (Months 55-57): Project Development & Paper Writing
└── Q4 (Months 58-60): Publication & Second Project Initiation

Year 5: Research Completion
├── Q1-Q2: Second Project & Additional Publications
├── Q3: Thesis Writing
└── Q4: Defense Preparation
```

### Year 4 Goals

**Primary Goals:**
1. Complete first original research project
2. Submit first peer-reviewed paper
3. Develop research independence
4. Establish professional network

**Secondary Goals:**
5. Present at one conference/seminar
6. Begin second research project
7. Develop advanced skills in specialization
8. Build collaborative relationships
