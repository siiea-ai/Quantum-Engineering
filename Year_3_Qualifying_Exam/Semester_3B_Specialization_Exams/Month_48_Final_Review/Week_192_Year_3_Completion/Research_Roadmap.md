## Part 9: Risk Management

### Timeline Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Project takes longer | Build buffer, have backup plan |
| Paper rejected | Revise quickly, resubmit |
| Results not novel | Verify literature early |
| Advisor unavailable | Build broader support network |
| Personal challenges | Maintain flexibility, seek support |

### Contingency Plans

**If Project 1 fails (Month 54):**
- Option A: Pivot to related problem
- Option B: Salvage partial results
- Option C: Start fresh with backup project

**If behind schedule (Month 66):**
- Reduce scope if needed
- Focus on completing 2 strong projects
- Consider publishing in faster venues
