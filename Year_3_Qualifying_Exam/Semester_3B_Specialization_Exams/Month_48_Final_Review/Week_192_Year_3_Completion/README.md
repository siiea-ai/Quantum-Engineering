## Transition Framework

### From Coursework to Research

| Coursework Phase | Research Phase |
|------------------|----------------|
| Learning established facts | Creating new knowledge |
| Following curriculum | Defining own direction |
| Solving known problems | Tackling open questions |
| Assessment by others | Self-directed progress |
| Broad coverage | Deep specialization |

### Key Mindset Shifts

1. **From Consumer to Producer**
   - You now contribute to the literature you once only read

2. **From Certainty to Uncertainty**
   - Research involves pursuing questions with unknown answers

3. **From Graded to Self-Evaluated**
   - Success is measured by contribution, not test scores

4. **From Individual to Collaborative**
   - Research involves community engagement

5. **From Learning to Discovery**
   - The goal shifts from understanding to extending
