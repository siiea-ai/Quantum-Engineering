## Assessment Framework

### Final Mock Exam Passing Criteria

#### Written Component (4 hours)
- **Passing Threshold:** 80% overall
- **Minimum per Section:** 70% (QM, QI/QC, QEC each)
- **Format:** 10 problems, full solutions required

| Section | Weight | Problems |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Quantum Mechanics | 35% | 3-4 problems |
| Quantum Information | 35% | 3-4 problems |
| Quantum Error Correction | 30% | 2-3 problems |

#### Oral Component (90 minutes)
- **Format:** Three phases
  1. Research proposal presentation (20 min)
  2. Broad questioning across all topics (40 min)
  3. Deep dive on specialization area (30 min)

- **Evaluation Criteria:**
  - Conceptual clarity and accuracy
  - Ability to think on feet with unknown questions
  - Connection between concepts
  - Research readiness indicators

## Key Formulas to Master (Final Review)

### Quantum Mechanics Essentials

$$\boxed{i\hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial t}|\psi\rangle = \hat{H}|\psi\rangle}$$

$$\boxed{\Delta A \cdot \Delta B \geq \frac{1}{2}|\langle[\hat{A}, \hat{B}]\rangle|}$$

$$\boxed{E_n^{(1)} = \langle n^{(0)}|\hat{H}'|n^{(0)}\rangle}$$

### Quantum Information Essentials

$$\boxed{S(\rho) = -\text{Tr}(\rho \log \rho)}$$

$$\boxed{F(\rho, \sigma) = \left(\text{Tr}\sqrt{\sqrt{\rho}\sigma\sqrt{\rho}}\right)^2}$$

$$\boxed{|\text{Bell}\rangle = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(|00\rangle + |11\rangle)}$$

### Quantum Error Correction Essentials

$$\boxed{P_a E_i^\dagger E_j P_a = \alpha_{ij} P_a \quad \text{(Knill-Laflamme)}}$$

$$\boxed{d = 2t + 1 \quad \text{(distance and correctable errors)}}$$

$$\boxed{p_L \sim \left(\frac{p}{p_{th}}\right)^{\lfloor(d+1)/2\rfloor}}$$

### Algorithm Complexity

$$\boxed{O(\sqrt{N}) \quad \text{(Grover search)}}$$

$$\boxed{O((\log N)^2 (\log \log N)(\log \log \log N)) \quad \text{(Shor factoring)}}$$
