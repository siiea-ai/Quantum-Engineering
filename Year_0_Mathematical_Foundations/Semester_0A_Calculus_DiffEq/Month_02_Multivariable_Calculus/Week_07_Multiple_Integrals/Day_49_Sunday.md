### Polar Coordinates
$$\iint_R f(x,y) \, dA = \iint f(r\cos\theta, r\sin\theta) \, r \, dr \, d\theta$$

### Triple Integrals

### Applications
- **Area:** $A = \iint_D 1 \, dA$
