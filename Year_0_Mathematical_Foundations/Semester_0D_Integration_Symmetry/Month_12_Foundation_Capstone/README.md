# Month 12: Foundation Capstone

## Overview

**Days 309-336 (28 days)**

Month 12 concludes Year 0: Mathematical Foundations with a comprehensive review and integration of all material covered over the past 11 months. This capstone month synthesizes calculus, linear algebra, differential equations, complex analysis, classical mechanics, functional analysis, scientific computing, and group theory into a unified foundation for quantum mechanics.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this month, you will:

1. Demonstrate mastery of all Year 0 mathematical concepts
2. Solve comprehensive problems integrating multiple topics
3. Complete a computational capstone project
4. Connect all mathematical tools to quantum mechanics
5. Be fully prepared for Year 1: Quantum Mechanics Core

## Weekly Structure

### Week 45: Mathematics Comprehensive Review (Days 309-315)
- Single and multivariable calculus synthesis
- Linear algebra from vectors to operators
- Differential equations and eigenvalue problems
- Complex analysis and special functions

### Week 46: Physics Comprehensive Review (Days 316-322)
- Classical mechanics: Lagrangian and Hamiltonian formulations
- Symmetry, conservation laws, and Noether's theorem
- Phase space, Poisson brackets, and canonical transformations
- Bridge to quantum mechanics

### Week 47: Computational Capstone Project (Days 323-329)
- Design and implement a comprehensive physics simulation
- Apply numerical methods from all Year 0 topics
- Visualization and analysis
- Documentation and presentation

### Week 48: Quantum Mechanics Preview (Days 330-336)
- Introduction to quantum concepts
- Mathematical structure of quantum mechanics
- Postulates and interpretation
- Year 1 roadmap and preparation
