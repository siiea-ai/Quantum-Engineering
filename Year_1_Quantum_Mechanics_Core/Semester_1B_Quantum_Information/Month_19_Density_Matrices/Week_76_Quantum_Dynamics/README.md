## Key Formulas

### von Neumann Equation
$$\frac{d\rho}{dt} = -\frac{i}{\hbar}[H, \rho]$$

### Quantum Channel (Kraus)
$$\mathcal{E}(\rho) = \sum_k K_k \rho K_k^\dagger, \quad \sum_k K_k^\dagger K_k = I$$

### Choi Matrix
$$J(\mathcal{E}) = (\mathcal{E} \otimes I)(|\Phi^+\rangle\langle\Phi^+|)$$
