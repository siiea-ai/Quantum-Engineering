## Key Protocols

### Teleportation
$$|\psi\rangle_C \otimes |\Phi^+\rangle_{AB} \xrightarrow{Bell + Pauli} |\psi\rangle_B$$

### Superdense Coding
$$1 \text{ qubit} + 1 \text{ ebit} \rightarrow 2 \text{ classical bits}$$

### Entanglement Swapping
$$|\Phi^+\rangle_{AB} \otimes |\Phi^+\rangle_{CD} \xrightarrow{Bell_{BC}} |\Phi^+\rangle_{AD}$$
