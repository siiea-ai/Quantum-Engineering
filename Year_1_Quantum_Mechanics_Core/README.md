## Key Topics Covered

### Quantum Mechanics (Semester 1A)
- Hilbert space formalism and Dirac notation
- Measurement postulate and uncertainty relations
- Time evolution (Schrödinger, Heisenberg, Interaction pictures)
- One-dimensional systems (QHO, square wells, tunneling)
- Angular momentum algebra and spin
- Hydrogen atom and fine structure
- Perturbation theory (time-independent and time-dependent)
- WKB approximation and Berry phase
- Identical particles and second quantization
- Scattering theory

### Quantum Information (Semester 1B)
- Density matrices and mixed states
- Quantum channels and CPTP maps
- Entanglement theory and Bell inequalities
- Quantum teleportation and superdense coding
- Single and multi-qubit gates
- Universal gate sets
- Quantum algorithms (Deutsch-Jozsa, Simon, QFT, Phase estimation, Shor, Grover)
- Variational algorithms (VQE, QAOA)
- Quantum error correction basics
- Stabilizer formalism

## From Year 0 to Year 1

### Mathematical Foundation → Quantum Application

| Year 0 Topic | Year 1 Application |
|--------------|-------------------|
| Linear Algebra | Hilbert space, operators |
| Complex Analysis | Wave functions, propagators |
| Differential Equations | Schrödinger equation |
| Classical Mechanics | Hamiltonian → Quantum |
| Functional Analysis | Unbounded operators |
| Group Theory | Angular momentum, symmetries |

### The Correspondence

```
Year 0: Classical                Year 1: Quantum
─────────────────               ─────────────────
Observable F(q,p)    →         Operator F̂
{F,G} = ∂F/∂q ∂G/∂p - ...  →   [F̂,Ĝ]/iℏ
Phase space          →         Hilbert space
H(q,p)               →         Ĥ
Deterministic        →         Probabilistic
```
