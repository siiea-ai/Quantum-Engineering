## Key Formulas

### Transition Probability
$$P_{i \to f}(t) = \frac{1}{\hbar^2}\left|\int_0^t V_{fi}(t')e^{i\omega_{fi}t'}\,dt'\right|^2$$

### Fermi's Golden Rule
$$\Gamma_{i \to f} = \frac{2\pi}{\hbar}|V_{fi}|^2\rho(E_f)$$

### Harmonic Perturbation Resonance
$$P_{i \to f} \propto \frac{\sin^2[(\omega_{fi} - \omega)t/2]}{(\omega_{fi} - \omega)^2}$$
