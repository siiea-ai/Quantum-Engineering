# Month 16: Three-Dimensional Problems

## Overview
**Days 421-448** | Year 1, Month 16 | Semester 1A: QM Foundations

This month applies the full formalism of quantum mechanics to realistic three-dimensional systems, culminating in the complete solution of the hydrogen atom with all its fine structure corrections.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this month, you will be able to:
1. Solve the radial Schrödinger equation for central potentials
2. Derive the complete hydrogen atom solution
3. Understand spin-orbit coupling and fine structure
4. Explain hyperfine structure and nuclear effects
5. Analyze atoms in external magnetic and electric fields
6. Connect atomic structure to quantum computing implementations
