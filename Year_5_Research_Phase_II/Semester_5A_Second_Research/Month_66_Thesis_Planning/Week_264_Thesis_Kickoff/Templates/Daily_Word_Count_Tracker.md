## Recovery Strategies

### When Behind

| Gap | Strategy |
|-----|----------|
| <500 words behind | Extra 15 min tomorrow |
| 500-1000 words behind | Weekend catch-up session |
| 1000-2000 words behind | Intensive writing day |
| >2000 words behind | Review and adjust weekly target |

### Preventing Burnout

- [ ] Take one full day off per week
- [ ] Limit intensive sessions to 4 hours
- [ ] Celebrate milestones
- [ ] Vary writing locations
- [ ] Maintain exercise and sleep

## Quick Reference

### Word Count Commands

**LaTeX (texcount)**:
```bash
texcount -sum thesis.tex
texcount -sub chapter1.tex
```

**Overleaf**:
- Menu → Word Count

**Markdown (command line)**:
```bash
wc -w *.md
```

### Conversion Reference

| Format | Words per page (approx) |
|--------|-------------------------|
| Double-spaced, 12pt | 250-300 |
| 1.5 spacing, 12pt | 350-400 |
| Single-spaced, 12pt | 500-600 |
