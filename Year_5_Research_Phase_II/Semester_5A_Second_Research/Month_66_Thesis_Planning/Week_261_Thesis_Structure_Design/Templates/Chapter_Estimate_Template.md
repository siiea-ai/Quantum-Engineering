# Chapter Estimate Template

## Thesis Page Count Planning and Tracking

---

## Overview

This template provides a detailed framework for estimating and tracking the page count for each chapter and section of your PhD thesis. Accurate estimation helps ensure balanced content distribution and realistic timeline planning.

---

## Estimation Guidelines

### Pages Per Element (Approximate)

| Element Type | Typical Length | Notes |
|--------------|----------------|-------|
| Full page of text | 1 page | ~300-350 words, double-spaced |
| Major figure with caption | 0.5-1 page | Including white space |
| Full-page figure | 1 page | Complex diagrams, data plots |
| Table (small-medium) | 0.25-0.5 page | 5-15 rows |
| Table (large) | 0.5-1 page | 15+ rows or complex |
| Equation block (1-3 eqs) | 0.1-0.2 page | With surrounding text |
| Major theorem + proof | 0.5-1 page | Depending on complexity |
| Algorithm pseudocode | 0.3-0.5 page | Including explanation |

### Writing Speed Benchmarks

| Writing Phase | Pages/Week | Notes |
|---------------|------------|-------|
| First draft (fast) | 10-15 | Rough content generation |
| First draft (careful) | 5-10 | More polished initial writing |
| Revision (major) | 15-20 | Significant restructuring |
| Revision (minor) | 20-30 | Copy editing, polish |
| Final formatting | 30-50 | Layout and proofreading |
