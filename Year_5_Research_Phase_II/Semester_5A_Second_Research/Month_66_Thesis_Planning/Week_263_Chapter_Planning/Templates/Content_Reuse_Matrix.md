## Reuse Guidelines

### What Can Be Reused Directly
- Your own published text (with permission if required)
- Figures you created (update formatting)
- Equations and derivations
- Data tables (update formatting)

### What Needs Adaptation
- Multi-author text (rewrite in your voice)
- Conference paper text (expand for thesis)
- Background sections (tailor to thesis scope)
- Discussion sections (broaden perspective)

### What Cannot Be Reused
- Co-author-written sections (without permission)
- Licensed figures from other papers
- Unpublished collaborator contributions

### Copyright Considerations
- [ ] Check journal policies on thesis reuse
- [ ] Document permissions obtained
- [ ] Add attribution statements where needed
- [ ] Verify figure licensing
