## Guidelines for Page Counts

### Pages Per Content Type

| Content | Typical Length |
|---------|----------------|
| 1 page of text | ~300-350 words (double-spaced) |
| 1 major figure + caption | ~0.5-1 page |
| 1 full-page figure | 1 page |
| 1 theorem + proof | ~0.5-1 page |
| 1 algorithm listing | ~0.5 page |
| 1 small table | ~0.25 page |
| 1 large table | ~0.5-1 page |
| 1 numbered equation block | ~0.1-0.2 page |

### Warning Signs

**Too Long**:
- Chapter exceeds max by >10%
- Total thesis approaching 250+ pages
- Background longer than research chapters

**Too Short**:
- Research chapter under 35 pages
- Discussion under 10 pages
- Total thesis under 140 pages
