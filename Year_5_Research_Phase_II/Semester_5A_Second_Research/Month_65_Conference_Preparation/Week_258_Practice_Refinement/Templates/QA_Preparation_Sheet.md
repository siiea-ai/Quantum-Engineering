## Quick Reference During Q&A

**Remember:**
1. **Listen** fully before responding
2. **Pause** briefly to collect thoughts
3. **Answer directly** first, then elaborate
4. **Stay calm** regardless of question tone
5. **Keep responses brief** (30-60 seconds)
6. **Offer to continue** conversation after session
