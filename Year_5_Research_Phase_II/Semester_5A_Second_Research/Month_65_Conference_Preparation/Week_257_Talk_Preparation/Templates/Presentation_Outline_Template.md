## Timing Contingency Plan

*If running long, what can you cut?*

| If at slide X at time Y | Cut/Speed through |
|------------------------|-------------------|
| Slide 10 at 12:00 | Speed through slide 11-12 |
| Slide 12 at 14:00 | Cut technical depth |
| Slide 14 at 16:00 | Truncate implications |

*Protected content (never cut):*
- Main result (Slides 8-9)
- Summary (Slide 17)

---

## Transition Phrases

**Motivation → Background:**
"To understand how we achieve this, let me briefly review..."

**Background → Results:**
"With that foundation, let me show you what we found..."

**Results → Implications:**
"So what does this mean for the field?"

**Implications → Summary:**
"Let me conclude by summarizing..."

---

## Post-Outline Checklist

- [ ] All three key takeaways are clearly represented
- [ ] Timing adds up to ~20 minutes
- [ ] Each slide has one main point
- [ ] Transitions between sections are clear
- [ ] Backup slides cover anticipated questions
- [ ] Contingency plan for running long is ready
