## Quick Reference - Error Formulas

**Standard Error of Mean:**
$$SE = \frac{s}{\sqrt{N}}$$

**Propagation (Addition/Subtraction):**
$$\sigma_z = \sqrt{\sigma_x^2 + \sigma_y^2}$$

**Propagation (Multiplication/Division):**
$$\frac{\sigma_z}{z} = \sqrt{\left(\frac{\sigma_x}{x}\right)^2 + \left(\frac{\sigma_y}{y}\right)^2}$$

**Weighted Average:**
$$\bar{x} = \frac{\sum_i w_i x_i}{\sum_i w_i}, \quad w_i = \frac{1}{\sigma_i^2}$$
