## Part 8: Success Level Definitions

### Overall Success Assessment

At project completion, overall success is classified as:

**Exceptional Success:**
- All minimum criteria met
- All target criteria met
- At least [X] stretch goals achieved
- Completed within timeline

**Target Success:**
- All minimum criteria met
- Most (â‰¥80%) target criteria met
- Completed within timeline or with minor delay

**Minimum Success:**
- All minimum criteria met
- Some target criteria met
- Completed within reasonable timeline extension

**Partial Success:**
- Most minimum criteria met
- Clear PhD contribution achieved
- Publication record acceptable

**Below Expectations:**
- Significant minimum criteria unmet
- Requires remediation plan
