## Level 5: Writing Quality

### Clarity

- [ ] Jargon minimized or defined
- [ ] Sentences are direct and clear
- [ ] Paragraphs have clear topic sentences
- [ ] One idea per paragraph
- [ ] Transitions connect ideas

### Conciseness

- [ ] No unnecessary words
- [ ] No redundant phrases
- [ ] No padding or filler
- [ ] Active voice preferred
- [ ] Appropriate paper length

### Technical Accuracy

- [ ] Equations correct
- [ ] Numbers and units consistent
- [ ] Terminology used correctly
- [ ] Acronyms defined on first use
- [ ] Symbols consistent throughout

### Grammar and Style

- [ ] Subject-verb agreement
- [ ] Consistent tense usage
- [ ] Proper punctuation
- [ ] No dangling modifiers
- [ ] Parallel construction in lists
