# Citation Verification Form

## Purpose

Use this form to systematically verify the accuracy of every citation in your thesis. Complete verification prevents embarrassing errors and demonstrates scholarly rigor.

---

## Verification Protocol

For each reference, verify the following against the original source:

1. **Authors**: Names spelled correctly, in correct order
2. **Year**: Publication year (not submission or arXiv year)
3. **Title**: Exact title, proper capitalization
4. **Venue**: Correct journal/conference name
5. **Volume/Pages**: Correct numbers
6. **DOI/arXiv**: Identifier correct and functional
7. **Claim Support**: Citation actually supports the claim made
