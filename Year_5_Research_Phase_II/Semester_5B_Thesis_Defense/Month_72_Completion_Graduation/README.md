## Post-PhD Pathways

### Academic Track

**Postdoctoral Research**
- Apply to top quantum research groups (IBM, Google, national labs, universities)
- Target 2-4 year postdoc to build independent research profile
- Focus on high-impact publications and grant writing
- Timeline: Apply 6-12 months before graduation

**Faculty Positions**
- Tenure-track positions at research universities
- Teaching-focused positions at liberal arts colleges
- Research scientist positions at national laboratories

### Industry Track

**Quantum Software Engineer**
- Companies: IBM Quantum, Google Quantum AI, Rigetti, IonQ, Quantinuum
- Roles: Algorithm development, compiler optimization, error correction implementation
- Skills emphasized: Programming, systems design, quantum algorithms

**Quantum Research Scientist**
- Corporate research labs: Microsoft Research, AWS Quantum, Google Research
- Focus on fundamental research with product applications
- Bridge between academic research and product development

**Technical Program Manager**
- Lead quantum computing initiatives
- Coordinate between research and engineering teams
- Requires technical depth plus management skills

### Entrepreneurship Track

**Startup Founder**
- Identify commercial applications of quantum technology
- Build team, secure funding, develop product
- Consider quantum software, consulting, or hardware

**Consulting**
- Advise companies entering quantum computing space
- Technology assessment and strategy development
- Technical due diligence for investors

### Ananta AGI Path

**Quantum-AI Integration Research**
- Quantum machine learning for AGI development
- Quantum optimization for neural architecture search
- Consciousness and quantum information theory
- Building towards beneficial artificial general intelligence
