# Discussion Section Template

## Purpose

This template provides a comprehensive structure for writing the discussion section of your thesis chapter. The discussion interprets your results, places them in context, acknowledges limitations, and articulates implications.

---

## Chapter N.5: Discussion

### N.5.1 Summary of Principal Findings

**Template:**

This chapter presented an investigation of [research topic] using [primary methodology]. The main findings are:

1. **[Finding 1 title]**: [One-sentence summary]. This result addresses [which research question/objective].

2. **[Finding 2 title]**: [One-sentence summary]. This finding [significance statement].

3. **[Finding 3 title]**: [One-sentence summary]. This [how it contributes].

The following sections discuss these findings in detail, compare them with prior work, acknowledge limitations, and explore implications.

**Guidance:**
- Length: 0.5-1 page
- Purpose: Orient reader to discussion structure
- Tone: Factual recap before interpretive analysis

---

### N.5.4 Limitations and Caveats

**Introduction:**

Several limitations of this work should be acknowledged to properly interpret the results and guide future research. We discuss these limitations not to undermine our conclusions, but to be transparent about the scope and boundaries of our findings.

## Discussion Quality Checklist

### Interpretation
- [ ] All major findings interpreted
- [ ] Interpretations well-supported
- [ ] Alternative interpretations considered
- [ ] Confidence appropriately calibrated
- [ ] Unexpected results addressed

### Comparison
- [ ] Quantitative comparison table included
- [ ] Key prior work compared in detail
- [ ] Agreements and discrepancies explained
- [ ] Context within field progress provided
- [ ] Fair and accurate representation of others' work

### Limitations
- [ ] Methodological limitations acknowledged
- [ ] Sample/system limitations discussed
- [ ] Scope limitations clear
- [ ] Overreach warnings included
- [ ] Balanced presentation (honest but not undermining)

### Implications
- [ ] Subfield implications articulated
- [ ] Broader implications discussed
- [ ] Application implications assessed
- [ ] Recommendations for others provided

### Future Directions
- [ ] Immediate extensions identified
- [ ] Medium-term directions outlined
- [ ] Long-term vision connected
- [ ] Future work motivated by this work's findings/limitations

### Thesis Integration
- [ ] Research questions addressed
- [ ] Literature review connected
- [ ] Other chapters linked
- [ ] Overall contribution stated
