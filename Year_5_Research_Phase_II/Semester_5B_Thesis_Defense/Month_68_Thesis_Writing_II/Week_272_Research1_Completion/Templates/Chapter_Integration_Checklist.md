## Part 6: Writing Quality

### 6.1 Clarity

- [ ] Sentences are clear and unambiguous
- [ ] Technical terms defined on first use
- [ ] Jargon minimized or explained
- [ ] Complex concepts broken down appropriately

### 6.2 Concision

- [ ] No unnecessary words
- [ ] No redundant statements
- [ ] Appropriate length for each section
- [ ] Filler phrases eliminated

### 6.3 Grammar and Style

- [ ] Subject-verb agreement throughout
- [ ] Consistent tense (past for methods/results, present for discussion)
- [ ] Active voice preferred where appropriate
- [ ] No dangling modifiers
- [ ] Parallel structure in lists

### 6.4 Flow

- [ ] Logical progression within paragraphs
- [ ] Clear paragraph topic sentences
- [ ] Smooth transitions between paragraphs
- [ ] Reader always knows where they are
