3. [ ] Clarify if needed
4. [ ] Acknowledge the question
5. [ ] Provide direct answer
6. [ ] Support with evidence
7. [ ] Connect to implications
8. [ ] Confirm questioner satisfied

## Quick Reference Card (Print for Defense Day)

```
DEFENSE DAY QUICK REFERENCE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PRE-DEFENSE
• Arrive 1 hour early
• Test ALL equipment
• Breathe, visualize, affirm

PRESENTING
• Speak slowly and clearly
• Make eye contact with everyone
• Watch your time
• Stay confident

Q&A
• Listen fully before responding
• Pause to think (it's okay!)
• "That's an excellent question..."
• It's okay to say "I don't know, but..."

IF ANXIOUS
1. Deep breath (4 in, 7 hold, 8 out)
2. Ground (feet on floor)
3. Affirm ("I know my research")
4. Continue (one question at a time)

YOU'VE GOT THIS.
```
