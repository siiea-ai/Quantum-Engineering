### Figure Checklist for Each Figure

- [ ] Resolution adequate (300+ dpi for raster)
- [ ] Size appropriate for page
- [ ] Not distorted (aspect ratio maintained)
- [ ] Labels readable (min 8pt)
- [ ] Axis labels complete with units
- [ ] Legend complete (if applicable)
- [ ] Caption positioned below
- [ ] Caption complete and descriptive
- [ ] Number format: Figure X.Y
- [ ] Referenced in text before appearing

### Figure Numbering Sequence

- [ ] Figures numbered sequentially within chapters
- [ ] No gaps in numbering
- [ ] No duplicate numbers
- [ ] Appendix figures: A.1, A.2, etc.
