## Pass 1: Content and Argument Review

### Research Questions
- [ ] Main research question clearly stated in introduction
- [ ] All sub-questions identified and addressed
- [ ] Questions answered in conclusion
- [ ] Scope appropriately defined

### Thesis Argument
- [ ] Central thesis/claim is clear
- [ ] Each chapter contributes to overall argument
- [ ] Claims are appropriately hedged
- [ ] Counter-arguments addressed
- [ ] Conclusion follows logically from evidence

### Evidence and Support
- [ ] All claims supported by evidence
- [ ] Evidence is appropriate (data, citations, derivations)
- [ ] No unsupported assertions
- [ ] Limitations of evidence discussed
- [ ] Alternative interpretations considered

### Contribution Clarity
- [ ] Novel contributions clearly stated
- [ ] Contributions distinguished from prior work
- [ ] Significance of contributions articulated
- [ ] Contributions substantiated in text

## Pass 6: Style and Clarity Review

### Writing Quality Checklist

**Voice and Tense:**
- [ ] Active voice preferred where appropriate
- [ ] Past tense for methods and results
- [ ] Present tense for established facts
- [ ] Future tense limited to future work section
- [ ] Consistent throughout

**Clarity:**
- [ ] Technical terms defined
- [ ] Jargon explained
- [ ] Sentences not overly complex
- [ ] No ambiguous pronouns
- [ ] Clear antecedents for "this," "it," "these"

**Precision:**
- [ ] Specific rather than vague language
- [ ] Quantitative rather than qualitative where possible
- [ ] Appropriate hedging (may, could, suggests)
- [ ] No exaggeration or overselling
- [ ] Limitations honestly stated

**Concision:**
- [ ] No unnecessary words
- [ ] No redundant phrases
- [ ] No repetition without purpose
- [ ] Appropriate use of abbreviations

### Common Issues Checked

- [ ] "Very," "really," "quite" removed or replaced
- [ ] "In order to" shortened to "to"
- [ ] "It is important to note that" removed
- [ ] "Due to the fact that" changed to "because"
- [ ] Nominalization reduced (e.g., "make a decision" â†’ "decide")
- [ ] Passive constructions minimized
- [ ] Run-on sentences broken up
- [ ] Sentence variety maintained
